Spaghetti Pie

Description

Spaghetti pie is a delightful Italian-inspired dish that transforms al dente spaghetti into a flavorful casserole. The spaghetti is mixed with eggs and cheese to create a sturdy base, layered with a savory filling featuring ingredients like seasoned ground meat, marinara sauce, and melted cheese. As it bakes, the pie forms a crispy golden crust while maintaining a tender interior, resulting in a harmonious blend of pasta, protein, and cheesy goodness that's perfect for family dinners or gatherings, offering both visual appeal and delicious comfort.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 lb ground beef or sausage
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook Spaghetti: Boil spaghetti following package instructions. Drain and set aside.
    2. Prepare Spaghetti Mix: In a bowl, mix eggs and grated Parmesan. Add cooked spaghetti, season with a pinch of salt and pepper, and mix well.
  2. Make Filling: Cook ground beef or sausage in a pan until browned. Drain fat. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Build the Pie: Grease a baking dish. Spread spaghetti mixture as the base. Pour meat and sauce over it. Sprinkle mozzarella on top.
  4. Bake: Pop it in a 350°F (175°C) oven for 25-30 mins, until it's bubbly and the cheese is golden.
  5. Serve: Let it cool a bit, garnish with basil if you like, and enjoy with your loved ones. It's a tasty meal for any occasion!
